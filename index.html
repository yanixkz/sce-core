Why SCE exists

Most AI agents can produce an answer. Few can clearly show why they chose it, what evidence mattered, whether the trajectory was reliable, and whether the next decision improved.

Decide

Rank candidate plans and choose the best next action.

Explain

Use decision backbone extraction to separate decision-carrying facts from dangling context.

Improve

Track local prediction error, compute reliability, remember outcomes, and adapt future choices.

Why it is different

Decision backbone extraction

forward  = nodes reachable from evidence
backward = nodes that can reach the target decision
backbone = forward ∩ backward
dangling = forward - backbone

Reliability-aware planning

base score
+ memory bias
+ remembered reliability
→ selected plan
