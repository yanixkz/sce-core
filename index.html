Why SCE exists

Most AI agents can produce an answer. Few can clearly show why they chose it, which facts mattered, whether the trajectory was reliable, and whether the next decision improved. SCE Core adds that missing layer.

Decide

Rank candidate plans and choose the best next action with explicit scoring logic.

Explain

Use decision backbone extraction to separate decision-carrying facts from dangling context.

Improve

Track local prediction error, compute reliability, remember outcomes, and adapt future choices.

What makes it different

Decision backbone
forward  = nodes reachable from evidence
backward = nodes that can reach the target decision
backbone = forward ∩ backward
dangling = forward - backbone

This is structural extraction of which nodes actually carried the decision.

Reliability-aware planning
base score
+ memory bias
+ remembered reliability
→ selected plan

SCE measures trajectory quality and feeds that back into future selection.
